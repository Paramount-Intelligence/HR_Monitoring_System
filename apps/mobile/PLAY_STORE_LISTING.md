## Full description

PIMS is the mobile companion for your organization’s workforce platform. Sign in securely and access the tools you need based on your role — whether you are an employee, manager, or administrator.

**For everyone**
- Secure sign-in with session protection
- Personal dashboard with key updates at a glance
- Check in and check out for attendance
- View attendance history and status
- Update your profile and profile photo
- Real-time team messaging
- Alerts and notifications for important updates
- Offline-aware experience with sync when connectivity returns

**For managers and team leads**
- Team overview and member insights
- Review and action approvals
- Team attendance visibility
- Team performance and summary reports

**For HR and administrators**
- Workforce reports and analytics (role-based)
- User and team management tools
- Leave, corrections, and operational workflows as permitted by your role

**Communication**
- Voice and video calls with colleagues (where enabled for your organization)
- Push notifications for messages, alerts, and approvals

Access is role-based. You only see data and actions your organization has authorized for your account.

PIMS connects to your organization’s hosted workforce platform. An active account issued by your employer is required.
